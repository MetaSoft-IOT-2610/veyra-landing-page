# Veyra: Connected Care

## Project Overview

**Veyra** is a digital platform designed to **improve the quality, coordination, and transparency of elder care** in both assisted living facilities and home environments.

In many care settings, communication between caregivers and families is often fragmented, and clinical information can be dispersed across different sources. Veyra addresses this problem by providing a **centralized, secure, and real-time system** that connects caregivers, institutions, and families in a single platform.

The goal is not only to streamline operations but also to ensure that residents receive consistent, high-quality care while giving families **visibility, trust, and peace of mind** through continuous access to relevant information.



## Key Features

Veyra focuses on delivering both **operational efficiency** for care providers and **transparency** for families through the following functionalities:

### Seamless Communication
Secure, real-time communication channels between caregivers and family members, including updates, alerts, and relevant notifications about the resident’s condition or daily activities.

### Real-Time Health Monitoring
Immediate access to essential health data such as vital signs, treatment progress, medication adherence, and clinical history, allowing families and professionals to stay informed at all times.

### Optimized Clinical Management
Centralized management of care processes, including medication scheduling, treatment plans, caregiver task assignments, and daily clinical notes, reducing errors and improving coordination.

### Reporting and Analytics
Interactive dashboards that provide key indicators such as care quality metrics, staff performance, incident tracking, and treatment compliance, enabling data-driven decision-making.

### Dual Plan Focus
- **Family Care Plan (Home Health Care):** Designed for families who need to monitor and manage the care of a loved one at home, with simplified tracking and communication tools.
- **Institutional Plan (Nursing Home):** Tailored for assisted living facilities, supporting the management of multiple residents, staff coordination, and operational workflows.

### Accessibility
Veyra is built with inclusivity in mind. The landing page incorporates descriptive **Alt Text** for images, ensuring that visually impaired users can understand visual content through screen readers.

### Internationalization (i18n)
The platform supports both **English (EN)** and **Spanish (ES)** through dynamic translation using `data-i18n` attributes, allowing a broader and more inclusive user base.



## Technologies Used

This landing page was developed prioritizing **performance, simplicity, and scalability**:

- **HTML5 & CSS3:** Provide a clean and semantic structure along with responsive and modern styling.
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** Handles interactivity (menus, accordions, plan switching) and dynamic content translation without relying on heavy frameworks.
- **Flexbox & CSS Grid:** Ensure a responsive and adaptable layout across different devices.
- **Font Awesome:** Enhances user experience through clear and intuitive iconography.
- **Google Fonts:** Implements modern typography (`Instrument Serif`, `Jost`, `Manrope`, `Rubik`) to improve readability and visual identity.



## Value Proposition

Veyra goes beyond simple digitalization by **creating a reliable and transparent ecosystem of care**. It empowers caregivers with better tools, supports institutions with efficient management systems, and provides families with continuous insight into their loved ones’ well-being.

By combining technology, accessibility, and user-centered design, Veyra contributes to a more **human, connected, and trustworthy elder care experience**.
