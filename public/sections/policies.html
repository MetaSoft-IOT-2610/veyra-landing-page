Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025 — Your privacy matters to us.

1. Overview

At Veyra, we value and protect the privacy of all users — including administrators, caregivers, and family members. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect information such as user account details, contact information, medical and care-related data, usage logs, and communication records. All data collection is limited to what is necessary to provide and improve our services.

3. How We Use Data

Data is processed to deliver the platform’s features, facilitate secure communication between caregivers and families, generate reports, and improve care quality. We do not use personal or medical data for marketing without explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures — including encryption at rest and in transit, secure authentication methods, and role-based access controls — to protect your information from unauthorized access and loss.

5. Sharing & Third Parties

Veyra does not sell or distribute personal or medical information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist with technical operations under strict confidentiality agreements and only as necessary to support the service.

6. User Rights

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information in accordance with applicable laws. For such requests, contact us at NovaPeruTech@gmail.com.

7. Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Specific retention periods depend on the type of data and applicable laws.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We are committed to transparency, trust, and ethical data management in every interaction with our users.
